# PhotoGem

Find and clean similar photos in your library. Compare side by side, keep the best shots, and free up storage.

## Features

- **Similar Photo Detection** — Analyzes your photo library and groups visually similar photos together
- **Side-by-Side Comparison** — Compare photos in filmstrip or grid layout with file size, resolution, and capture date
- **Quality Scores** — Helps you pick the best shot from each group
- **Location Grouping** — Group similar photos by where they were taken
- **Date Range Filter** — Focus on specific time periods in your library
- **EXIF & Metadata** — View detailed photo information including camera settings and location

## Privacy

PhotoGem works entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded or shared. All analysis happens locally.

## Platforms

- macOS 14+
- iPadOS 17+
